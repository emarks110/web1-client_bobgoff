Design Challenge

Laying out the design challenge was the first step to truly getting to know my client Bob Goff. I accessed the IDEO Human Centered Design Toolkit to help guide me throught the design challenge process.

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

Organzing and helping users understand who Bob Goff is and his non-profit.

The Design Question:

How can the website be more organized and display his different books, organization, and programs in a well thought out way that benefits the user?

The Ultimate Impact

To impact others by showing them their gorls and how to love and be loved greater.

Possible Solutions

  • Hierachy
  • High-Quality images (othr than his face)
  • Organization
  • Simpler to Nagivate through (less complex)
  • Display the different ways he is helping his community
  • Make all different websites into one concise websites

Context and Constraints

Range of different countries and people in need from needing food or help making their dream come true. Wide range of people needing help and/or guidance which is hard to follow and keep up with all the different aspects.

The Design Question Restated

"How can the re-design of this website attract users to Bob Goff's organization and books?"
